Where I work

I'm a founder of PixieEngine . We make it easy for people to leverage their existing skills to develop HTML5 games in the browser. PixieEngine provides tools for creating pixel art, animations, and maps and integrates them into a browser based IDE with simple, elegant scripting APIs.

I also contract with PollEverywhere . They make awesome software to allow real time audience polling at events.
